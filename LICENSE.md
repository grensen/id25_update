```
IV. WHY AGPL? THE STRATEGIC CHOICE
================================================================================
```

**This is not just a software license. This is a defensive protocol.**

The AGPL-3.0 was chosen specifically because:

1. **It prevents "Mind Theft"** ‚Äì If someone builds an AI system using your logic to calculate human elimination as optimal, they MUST show their source code

2. **It closes the SaaS loophole** ‚Äì Running your protocol on a server and offering it as a service counts as distribution. They must release their modifications.

3. **It forces transparency** ‚Äì In an age where AI models are trained on human cognition without disclosure, AGPL mandates reciprocity

4. **It enables commercial use** ‚Äì You can sell ID25-based services. But you cannot hide the logic in a black box.

**The Choice:**
- MIT/Apache: "Take and close the source" ‚Üí **Enables the Extraction Model**
- GPL: "Share if you distribute binaries" ‚Üí **SaaS companies evade this**
- AGPL: "Share if ANYONE uses it over a network" ‚Üí **No escape**

If we are the training data, **we own the terms of extraction.**
